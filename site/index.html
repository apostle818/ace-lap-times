Philosophy

Why self-hosted?

Own your data

Your data lives on your hardware. No third party can access it, sell it, or shut it down.

No subscriptions

Deploy once, run forever. No monthly fees, no feature tiers, no accounts with us.

Open source

All code is MIT licensed. Read it, fork it, modify it, contribute back.

Docker-first

Every app ships as a Docker Compose stack. One command to deploy, one to update.
